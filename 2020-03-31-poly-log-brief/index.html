推导

$$
\begin {split}
B(x) & \equiv \ln A(x) \pmod {x ^ n} \
B’(x) & \equiv \dfrac {A’(x)} {A(x)} \pmod {x ^ n} \
B(x) & \equiv \int \dfrac {A’(x)} {A(x)} \pmod {x ^ n} \
\end {split}
$$
